About Us


















Calle Felize Pizza

During the start of the pandemic, specifically on the July of 2020, when the quarantine rules were stricter than ever,
we were really on a slump because there were nothing to do at all. So as a past time, I decided to make my own pizza recipe as a “meryenda”.

All the family members absolutely loved the new recipe and suggested to sell it online because “it was just that delicious”.
And so, feeling really confident with the recipe, we did try selling it online.
Fortunately, it immediately boomed and during the first week,
we decided to buy a new oven to compensate the capacity.

We managed the business as home made products for a year, then decided expand with a physical store on August of 2021.

Me and my wife are the ones running the business but other family members also decided to invest.
